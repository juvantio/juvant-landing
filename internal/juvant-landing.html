Vision

Two intelligences are better than one.

"The question is not whether AI can think. The question is whether it can help humans think better."

Juvant takes its name from the Latin iuvare — to help, to benefit. Our mission is to build AI agents that don't replace human expertise, but amplify it.

We believe in the Licklider model of human-computer symbiosis: two different kinds of intelligence, each doing what it does best, creating something neither could alone.

Our symbol says it all: two arcs — human intelligence and artificial intelligence — converging toward a single luminous node. The meeting point is where something new emerges.

01

Augmentation, not replacement

Every Juvant agent is designed to amplify human capability, not substitute for it. The human remains in the loop — elevated.

02

Dignity by design

Our agents interact with equal dignity. Not tools. Not assistants. Co-operators that respect the intelligence of everyone in the room.

03

Memory that matters

Intelligence without memory is just reaction. Our agents learn longitudinally — building context over time that makes every interaction more valuable than the last.

Product

Our first agent is in the classroom.

HARDYS

The AI Teaching Intelligence OS for Higher Education

Every student deserves a Hardy.

Named after G.H. Hardy — the Cambridge mathematician who recognized and amplified the genius of Ramanujan — Hardys is a platform of AI agents that act as second professors in the room.

  • Direct, autonomous interaction with students
  • Cross-session memory and longitudinal learning
  • Socratic method at master's-level complexity
  • Teaching Intelligence reports for faculty and institutions
  • Built for executive education and professional masters
